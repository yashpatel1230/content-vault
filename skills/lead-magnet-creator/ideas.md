# Lead-magnet ideas · 30+ creative angles

A vault of 5 PDFs is the obvious starting point. Be more creative, be
more generous. This file is the brainstorming menu. Pick one, run it
through the skill.

Each idea has: **Title** · **Format** · **ICP** · **Outcome promise** ·
**Anchor proof / source material**.

Format codes: `Notion` (Notion page) · `GDoc` (Google Doc) · `PDF` ·
`Sheet` (Google Sheet) · `Skills` (Claude Code Skills repo) · `GitHub`
(starter repo) · `GPT` (custom GPT) · `Web` (small interactive web app) ·
`Video` (Loom / YouTube / Vimeo) · `Vault` (bundle of several).

**Format is a decision, not a default.** Pick the format that fits the
content.

---

## Tier 1 · Highest leverage (build these first)

Pull these from your strongest stories or your launch narrative. Low
risk, high anchor strength.

### 1. The 80k-Emails Playbook · `PDF` + `GitHub`
- **ICP**: B2B founders, agency owners running cold email
- **Promise**: Book 85 sales calls a month from 80,000 cold emails on a
  Claude-Code-built stack
- **Anchor**: Your strongest cold-email post (engagement + receipts).
  Stack: Claude Code, Trigger.dev, Supabase, an email-finder, an
  email-verifier, Instantly (or your equivalent).
- **Build**: PDF that walks the system end-to-end + a public GitHub
  repo with the actual scripts.

### 2. The Outbound Stack Picker · `Sheet` + `PDF`
- **ICP**: founders evaluating which outbound stack to commit to
- **Promise**: Decide between two competing stacks in 10 minutes based
  on your volume, ICP, and budget
- **Anchor**: Document the two stacks you'd recommend (you've probably
  walked clients through this on calls).
- **Build**: Sheet with input fields (volume / ICP-list-source /
  monthly budget) and a recommended stack output, plus a 4-page PDF
  explaining why. Real prices inline.

### 3. The GTM Signal Library · `Notion` + `PDF`
- **ICP**: GTM engineers and outbound operators
- **Promise**: 50+ buying signals with where to source each, how to
  score it, and what to do when it fires
- **Anchor**: Your strongest "intent signals will make or break
  outbound" post. Pull from PredictLeads, Trigify, Bombora, UserGems,
  customer-installed-tech, hiring signals, funding rounds.
- **Build**: Notion DB rows with columns (signal · source · cost ·
  trigger threshold · suggested action · example sequence). PDF wraps
  with a top-10 highlight reel.

### 4. The 7-Day AI CRO Build-Along · `PDF` + `GitHub` + `GPT`
- **ICP**: founders wanting their own autonomous AI CRO
- **Promise**: Build a working AI CRO clone in 7 days using Claude Code
- **Anchor**: Your launch / V2 announcement narrative. The vault becomes
  the manual for how you built the product, not a sales pitch for it.
- **Build**: 7-page PDF, one page per day. Companion repo with Claude
  Code prompts per day. Custom GPT for "AI CRO Architect" coaching.

### 5. The LinkedIn Profile Audit GPT · `GPT` + `PDF`
- **ICP**: solopreneurs / agency owners who post on LinkedIn
- **Promise**: Get your profile audited and rewritten in 5 minutes
- **Anchor**: If you have an existing audit GPT, link it. Otherwise
  build it.
- **Build**: GPT in your brand voice + PDF wrapper that explains how to
  use it and what to expect.

## Tier 4 · Wild cards (creative, less obvious)

### 26. The "Send Me Your Cold Email" Auto-Audit · `Web` + `GPT`
- **Promise**: Send your cold email to `audit@<YOUR_DOMAIN>` and get an
  AI roast back in 60 seconds
- **Mechanic**: route the email through a Trigger.dev workflow + Claude
  API. Reply with a structured roast. Each reply links back to the
  public PDF "How to write a cold email I wouldn't roast".
- **Why this works**: free distribution, novelty, and the magnet is
  delivered _to_ the reader's inbox in their actual workflow.

### 27. The "Cold Email Battle Royale" Bracket · `Web` + `Sheet`
- **Promise**: Pit your cold email against a Claude-rewritten version. Vote.
- **Mechanic**: small public web tool. User pastes email. Tool generates
  4 rewrites in 4 different creator voices. User votes the best. Tool
  emails the winning version + a CTA.
- **Bonus**: this is a content engine. Each round is shareable.

### 28. The "Audit Your Founder LinkedIn" GPT-Pack · `GPT × 5`
- **Promise**: 5 GPTs that audit your LinkedIn (profile, banner, headline, posts, comments) for $0
- **Why**: each GPT is its own indexed asset on the GPT store. 5x distribution surface.

### 29. The Daily GTM News Brief · `Email` + `Notion`
- **Promise**: 5 GTM signals + 1 hot take, daily, 5am ET
- **Mechanic**: cron job + Notion source DB + email broadcast (Loops,
  Beehiiv, etc.)
- **Why**: not a one-off magnet, a habit. Highest LTV form factor on
  this list.

### 30. The "Steal My Outbound Calendar" iCal · `iCal feed` + `PDF`
- **Promise**: Subscribe to your outbound rhythm. See exactly when each
  campaign sends, follows up, and rests.
- **Mechanic**: a public iCal URL. Subscribers see "Send LinkedIn batch
  · Cohort A · Day 1" etc. Live and updating.
- **Why**: the only magnet on this list that lives _in the reader's
  calendar_ and reminds them to ship.

### 31. The Anti-Onboarding Onboarding · `Loom` + `PDF`
- **Promise**: A 90-second Loom explaining what your product is, sent
  30 seconds after signup
- **Why this works**: it's not a magnet on the page, it's a magnet in
  the inbox. Pattern lifted from Loom-led onboarding flows. 90 seconds.
  Ends with a Cal CTA.

### 32. The Customer Acquisition Calculator · `Web` + `Sheet`
- **Promise**: Punch in your ARPU, lifetime, conversion rate. See how
  much you can spend on outbound to break even.
- **Mechanic**: 8 input fields, 1 output. Public web tool. Embedded
  calculator on `<YOUR_DOMAIN>/free`.

### 33. The "Office Hours" Lottery · `Cal` + `PDF`
- **Promise**: 1 free 20-minute call, weekly. Apply by submitting your

- **Mechanic**: a Cal slot per week. Apply via Tally form. You pick one.

### `Skills` (Claude Code Skills repo · installable into reader's `~/.claude/skills/`)
- **Brand GTM Skills vault repo**: package up the public-shareable
  patterns from your repo. Candidates the reader installs:
  - `/cold-email`: drafts a cold email in your voice (banned-words
    list, real subject-line patterns, real first-line hooks).
  - `/icp-audit`: scores a website / LinkedIn profile against a built-in
    ICP scorecard. Outputs a 0-100 score + suggested fixes.
  - `/gtm-stack-picker`: same logic as idea #2 (Outbound Stack Picker)
    but as an installable skill.
  - `/anti-slop`: rewrites a draft to remove the AI-slop list (mirrors
    your `linkedin-copywriter` blacklist).
  - `/signal-scan`: takes a domain, returns a list of buying signals
    detected (uses PredictLeads / Trigify / hiring page scrape).
- Single-skill repos: each of the above can also ship as a standalone
  repo for narrow distribution.
- This format is the most operator-grade magnet for the GTM-engineering
  ICP. Reader runs `git clone … ~/.claude/skills/<name>` and instantly
  has your patterns.

## Pillar mapping (Notion Content DB · Pillar field)

When logging the magnet's Notion row, pick:

- **Educational / Tactical**: most magnets (1-15, 18-22, 23-25, 30-32)
- **Building in Public**: 16, 20, 25, 33
- **Promotional**: 16 (Operating System) only · others are giveaways
- **Trend Insights**: 17 (Annual GTM Trends), 29 (Daily news brief)
- **Personal**: only if the magnet is a personal story (rare for this
  format)
- **Memes**: skip · use `/satire-short` instead

If a magnet straddles pillars, pick the dominant one. The Notion field
allows multi-select so secondary pillars can be added.
